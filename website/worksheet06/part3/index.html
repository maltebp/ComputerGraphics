The texture filter used is LINEAR_MIPMAP_LINEAR for minification and LINEAR for magnification. I've chosen a full linear model due the color variance of the texture image. The linear texel filtering allows for smoother transistions during rotation (no flickering), and smoother texture when scaling. The linear mipmap lookup makes the texture quality "jump" less when zooming in and out.
