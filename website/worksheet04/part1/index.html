Subdivisions

FPS: ?

Drawing a Sphere by using subdivision, and (adjusted) triangle surface normals for colors to gain a better sense of the subdivision. The subdivision level goes from 0 to 8 inclusive, and increments by 1.
Note: The subdivions is not implemented in a super fast manner, so increasing the level to the last to levels is really slow. Also, the sphere vertices are "uploaded" to the vertex buffer every frame, so FPS drops a bit.
